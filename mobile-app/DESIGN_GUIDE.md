## 🎨 Design Features

### Colors
- **Primary**: `#0066ff` (Blue)
- **Accent**: `#00d4ff` (Cyan)
- **Background**: `#0f0f16` (Dark)
- **Cards**: `#1a1a24` (Darker)
- **Text**: `#ffffff` (White)
- **Secondary**: `#8a8a9e` (Gray)

### Animations
✨ Smooth transitions between screens  
✨ Gradient overlays on cards  
✨ Bottom tab indicators  
✨ Button hover effects  

### Typography
- **Titles**: Bold, 20-32px
- **Subtitles**: Medium, 14-18px
- **Body**: Regular, 12-14px
- **System Font**: Inter (fallback to system)

---

## 📊 Size Comparison

### Mobile Sizes Supported
- Small phones: 320px (compact layout)
- Standard: 375px (iPhone SE)
- Large: 414px+ (Plus models)
- Extra large: 600px+ (tablets - portrait)
- Tablets: 768px+ (landscape)
